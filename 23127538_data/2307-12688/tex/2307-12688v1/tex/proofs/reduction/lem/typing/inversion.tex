    $\SesEnv=\SesEnv',\PrcP:\CIso;$

    and $\PrcMsg_i=\MsgType_j$
    and $\AsSet[j][k]$

    \IPJudgement*<[\ICalcRecv[\PrcMsg_i].{\Prc_i}<{\PrcAfter}]>:{\PrcP:\CIso;[\TypInteract|_[k]]}\ %
    %
