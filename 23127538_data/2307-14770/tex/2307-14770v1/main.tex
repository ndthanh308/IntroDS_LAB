  Our 3DPortraitGAN can generate full-head 3D avatars and output portrait images (a and d) of a single identity using camera poses and body poses from reference images (c and f). The real reference images (c and f) are sampled from our \textit{$\it{360}^{\circ}$PHQ} dataset. Shapes (b and e) are iso-surfaces extracted from the density field of each portrait using marching cubes. 
  For all results shown in this figure, we use truncation with $\psi=0.7$
  %, condition the generator on the average camera parameters and neutral body pose,
