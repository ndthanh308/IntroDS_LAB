% Abstract -----------------------------------------------
\begin{abstract}
\noindent
The first observation and study of two new baryonic structures in the final state $\Xibz\pip\pim$ and the confirmation of the \XibmstarstarB state in the $\Xibm\pip\pim$ decay mode are reported using proton-proton
collision data collected by the \lhcb experiment, corresponding to an integrated luminosity of 9\invfb. In addition, the properties of the 
known \Xibzstar, \Xibmprime and \Xibmstar resonances are measured with  improved precision. The new decay mode of the \Xibz baryon to the \Xicp\pim\pip\pim final state is observed and exploited for the first time in these measurements.



\end{abstract}
