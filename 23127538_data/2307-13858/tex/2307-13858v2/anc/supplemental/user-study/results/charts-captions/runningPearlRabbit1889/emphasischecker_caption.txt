This chart shows median single-family home prices in King County from 2005 to 2016 in a monthly manner. The median price rapidly increases from January 2015 to March 2018. After the peak occurs in March 2008 at the price of 450k USD, it decreases until May 2012 to the price of 300K USD. It then increases back until December 2015 to the maximum price of 487K USD.
