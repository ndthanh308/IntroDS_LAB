On Mar 01, 2020, president Macron's approval rate hit its peak after Dec 01, 2018 which was the lowest approval rate during his presidency. Starting from June of 2017 which was the start of his presidency, the approval rate continuously declined until Dec 2018 except the last quarter of 2017. However, the approval rate increased from Dec 2018 even though there had been a slight decrease of his approval rate from Dec 2019 to Jan 2020. The most rapid increase of the approval rate took place from February to March of 2020; the approval rate increased from 32.0%p to 43.0%p only in a month.
