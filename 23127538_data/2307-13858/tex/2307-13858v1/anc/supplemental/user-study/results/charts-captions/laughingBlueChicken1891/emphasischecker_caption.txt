This graph shows the weekly U.S. filed production of crude oil from 2006 to 2020. The production amount was nearly constant from 2006 to 2012 but it starts to increase around 1000 Barrels per day/one year from 2011 to 2015. The trend was reversed at 2015, but it starts to increase again in 2016 and showed nearly similar inclination with the previous growth from 2011 to 2015.
