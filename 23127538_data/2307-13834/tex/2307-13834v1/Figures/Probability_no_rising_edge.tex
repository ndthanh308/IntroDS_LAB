&\quad + p_1 \cdot p_2 \cdot p_4 \cdot (1 - p_3) \\
&\quad + p_1 \cdot p_2 \cdot p_3 \cdot (1 - p_4) \\

P(\text{Four rising edges}) &= p_1 \cdot p_2 \cdot p_3 \cdot p_4 \\
