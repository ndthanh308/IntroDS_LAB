\section{Training Details}
Similar to Flare7K and Wu \etal \ , we use a U-Net for training as a baseline. The difference is that compared with their apporaches that segments light sources, perform flare removal and image blending to merge
the results back into the segmented images, we did not use such a blending approach because we notice that it may lead to inaccurate classification on flare and also worse performance in restoration quality. Instead, we use a U-Net for predict the flare area and substract it from the input for predicting a flare-free image.
